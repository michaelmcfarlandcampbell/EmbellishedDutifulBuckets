Order of St Benedict

For many years, I have had an interest in monasticism and in particular the Order of St Benedict. In Autumn of 2020, I became an Oblate of the House of Initia Nova OSB.

Daily office

Each day, I try to pray at least in the morning, at midday, and in the evening.

The times for these prayers are:

  • 07:00 a.m. Matins (morning prayer)
  • 12:30 p.m. or 13:00 p.m. Sext (midday prayer)
  • 06:00 p.m. Vespers (evening prayer)

I would appreciate it if these times are respected.
